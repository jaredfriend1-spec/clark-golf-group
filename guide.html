No more messy email chains or forgetting who is playing what tees. Sign up in seconds and see the results instantly.

1 Pick Your Name

Tap + Sign up, search for your name, and tap it. Your selection locks in with a green confirmation chip so you always know who you're signing up as.

For the Admins

The app does the heavy lifting, math, and sorting for you. You just drag, drop, and push the buttons.

1 Set Up Your Days

The Admin tab opens straight to Manage Days — the first thing you need. Click Set up Saturday (or any day) and a step-by-step wizard walks you through setting the tee time, locking sign-ups, and generating teams.

2 Build Teams: Auto or Manual

You have two options for building teams:

Auto-generate sorts the roster by handicap and evenly distributes A, B, C, and D players into balanced groups with one click.

Manual build lets you pick how many players per team (3, 4, or 5), creates empty groups, and lets you hand-pick each player from a searchable list. Perfect when you want specific matchups.

Team builder - Saturday (16 players)

3 Fine-Tuning & Reordering

Want to swap a player to a different team? Just grab the drag handle (⋮) next to their name and drop them into a new group. Need to change who tees off first? Use the Group Dropdown in the header.

4 The Blind Draw

Have an uneven number of players? The app automatically detects this and adds a "Blind Draw" slot to the short group. You can manually assign any player from another group to fill the slot.

5 Publish & Lock

Once you are happy with the groups, hit Publish & Lock Teams. It assigns the official names (Team One, Team Two) and locks the roster.

Still love sending the weekly email?

No problem! Once teams are published, just hit the "Copy teams" button. It formats perfectly so you can paste it right into Gmail or iMessage.

6 Made a Mistake? Reset Teams

If something goes wrong after publishing — wrong player, bad grouping, whatever — just tap Reset Teams. It unpublishes and reopens the team builder so you can rebuild. All player sign-ups stay intact, nobody has to re-sign-up.

Sign-ups are always safe

Reset Teams only clears the team groupings. It never deletes player sign-ups. You can rebuild as many times as you need.

7 Payment Tracking

At the course, use the Scoring tab to collect money. A quick tracker lets you mark off who has paid their $25 with a single tap.

8 Instant Scoring & Payouts

Enter the team scores (Front, Back, Overall) and tap the Skins buttons. The app instantly calculates the total pot, splits ties, and shows exact dollar payouts per player.

9 End of Week Closeout

When the weekend is over, click End Week & Start Fresh in the Admin panel. The app saves all games to History, updates the Season Leaderboard, pushes player attendance to the Season Attendance tracker, and resets the board for next week.

Season Attendance

A separate tracker that follows every player across the whole season — how many rounds they've played and the year-end tournament pot.

1 Automatic Tracking

Every time a round is moved to history or the week is closed out, the players from that round are automatically pushed to the attendance tracker. No extra work needed.

2 Season Standings

A searchable leaderboard ranked by rounds played. Tap any player to see every date they played. Gold, silver, and bronze badges for the top three.

3 Year-End Tournament Pot

The pot tracks $1 per player per round automatically. It's always visible in the header so everyone knows the stakes.

4 Admin Controls

Admins (password protected) can manually log rounds, edit or delete existing rounds, import data from CSV, and archive seasons at year-end. Players can view everything but can't edit.
