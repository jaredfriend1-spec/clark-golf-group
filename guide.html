How It Works

For the Players

No more messy email chains or forgetting who is playing what tees. Sign up in seconds.

1 Pick Your Days & Tees

Just select your name, check the days you want to play, and choose your tees and handicap. Done.

2 Bringing a Guest? Easy.

Toggle over to "Guest", type in their name and handicap, and they are instantly added to the roster.

3 See the Teams Live

Once the admin publishes the groups, check the Teams tab to see exactly who you are grouped with, what position they are in, and the total team handicap.

For the Admins

The app does the heavy lifting, math, and sorting for you. You just push the buttons.

1 The Setup Wizard

Setting up a day is completely foolproof. Set the tee time, lock the sign-ups so the roster is frozen, and generate the groups in three clicks.

2 Auto-Balancing Teams

Hit "Auto-generate" and the app sorts the entire roster by handicap and evenly distributes A, B, C, and D players into groups.

Team builder - Saturday

3 Publish & Lock

Once you are happy with the groups, set the tee order using the Dropdowns. Hit Publish & Lock to instantly assign names (Team One, Team Two) and push the groups live to the players.

Still love sending the weekly email?

No problem! Once teams are published, just hit the "Copy teams" button. It instantly formats perfectly so you can paste it right into Gmail or iMessage.

4 Instant Scoring & Payouts

In the Scoring tab, enter the team scores (Front, Back, Overall) and tap the Skins buttons. The app instantly calculates the total pot, tracks who has paid, splits ties evenly, and calculates exact dollar payouts per player.
