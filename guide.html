For the Players

No more messy email chains or forgetting who is playing what tees. Sign up in seconds and see the results instantly.

1 Pick Your Name

Tap + Sign up, search for your name, and tap it. Your selection locks in with a green confirmation chip so you always know who you are signing up as.

For the Admins

The app does the heavy lifting, math, and sorting for you. You just drag, drop, and push the buttons.

1 Set Up Your Days

The Admin tab opens straight to Manage Days — the first thing you need. Click Set up Saturday (or any day) and a step-by-step wizard walks you through setting the tee time, locking sign-ups, and generating teams.

2 Auto-Balancing Teams

Hit "Auto-generate" and the app sorts the entire roster by handicap and evenly distributes A, B, C, and D players into groups.

Team builder - Saturday

3 Fine-Tuning & Reordering

Want to swap a player to a different team? Just grab the drag handle (⋮) next to their name and drop them into a new group. Need to change who tees off first? Use the Group Dropdown in the header.

4 The Blind Draw

Have an uneven number of players? The app automatically detects this and adds a "Blind Draw" slot to the short group. You can manually assign any player from another group to fill the slot.

5 Publish & Lock

Once you are happy with the groups, hit Publish & Lock Teams. It instantly assigns the official names (Team One, Team Two) and locks the roster so nobody can change it.

Still love sending the weekly email?

No problem! Once teams are published, just hit the "Copy teams" button. It formats perfectly so you can paste it right into Gmail or iMessage.

6 Payment Tracking

When you are at the course, use the Scoring tab to collect money. A quick tracker lets you mark off who has paid their $25 with a single tap.

7 Instant Scoring & Payouts

Enter the team scores (Front, Back, Overall) and tap the Skins buttons. The app instantly calculates the total pot, splits ties evenly, and calculates exact dollar payouts per player.

8 End of Week Closeout

When the weekend is over, just click End Week & Start Fresh in the Admin panel. The app automatically grabs all the games, securely saves them to History, updates everyone's Season Leaderboard stats, and perfectly resets the board for Monday.
