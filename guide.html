For the Admins

The app does the heavy lifting. You just drag, drop, and push the buttons.

1 Set Up Your Days

The Admin tab opens straight to Manage Days. Click Set up Saturday and a step-by-step wizard walks you through setting the tee time, locking sign-ups, and building teams. Day checklists track your progress in a collapsible section below.

Set up Saturday

2 Build Teams: Auto or Manual

Auto-generate sorts the roster by handicap and distributes A, B, C, and D players into balanced groups with one click. Pick team size (3, 4, or 5) and the app shows you the exact breakdown before committing.

Manual build lets you create empty groups and hand-pick each player from a searchable list.

4 Smart Team Mixing

The auto-generate algorithm checks your round history and avoids putting the same players together week after week. Over time, everyone naturally rotates through different groups while still keeping teams balanced by handicap.

Gets smarter over time

The more rounds in your history, the better the mixing works. Early on with little data, it focuses on handicap balance. As history builds up, it increasingly prioritizes fresh matchups.

5 Fine-Tuning & Reordering

Drag the handle (⋮) next to any player's name to move them between teams. Use the Group Dropdown in each team header to change the tee-off order. Works in both auto and manual modes.

7 Publish & Lock

8 Edit or Reset Published Teams

Need a last-minute change after publishing? Two options:

Edit Teams — unlocks the existing teams for rearranging. Drag players, reorder groups, then re-publish. No regenerating needed.

Reset Teams — clears all teams and starts over from scratch. Sign-ups are always preserved — nobody has to re-sign-up either way.

9 Roster Management

The admin can manage the full player roster from the Admin tab:

Edit names — tap "edit" next to any player to correct a spelling or update a name. Changes appear everywhere instantly.

Delete players — remove a player from the roster. Base roster members are hidden (and can be restored later). Custom-added players are permanently removed.

Add players — add new members or guests with name, tees, and handicap.

Nothing is permanent

Deleted base roster members appear in a "Hidden players" section at the bottom. Tap "Restore" to bring them back anytime.

Weekly Email

Generate a polished weekly update email and copy it to paste into Gmail. No email service needed — the app builds the email for you.

1 Open the Email Tab

Tap the envelope icon in the admin nav. The tab shows only upcoming play days — past days are excluded automatically.

Weekly Email

2 Edit the Subject Line

The subject auto-generates from the current week dates (e.g., "Clark Golf — Week of May 3-4 Update"). Edit it inline to customize before copying.

3 Add Day Notes

Each upcoming day has an optional note field (560 characters). Use it for game format, reminders, or anything the group needs to know. Notes auto-save as you type and show up in the email as a gold-accented "Note from the admin" callout. Leave it blank and it won't appear.

Note from the admin Game today: 2-man best ball, $5 skins on the side. Meet at the range by 1:00.

4 Live Preview

A scrollable preview at the bottom shows exactly what recipients will see — teams, sign-up lists, notes, and all. Updates in real time as you make changes.

5 Copy & Send

Two copy options at the bottom:

Copy Email — copies the full styled email. Paste into Gmail and it looks perfect with all the formatting, team cards, and colors.

Plain Text — copies a clean text-only version with ASCII formatting. Use this for iMessage, group texts, or email clients that mangle HTML.

Notes clear automatically

Season Attendance

A separate tracker that follows every player across the whole season — rounds played and the year-end tournament pot.

1 Automatic Tracking

Every time a round is moved to history or the week is closed out, player names are automatically pushed to attendance. No duplicate counting — if the same date is pushed twice, it replaces rather than duplicates.

2 Season Standings

A searchable leaderboard ranked by rounds played with a search bar at the top. Tap any player to see every date they played. Gold, silver, and bronze badges for the top three.

3 Year-End Tournament Pot

Tracks $1 per player per round automatically, always visible in the header.

4 Admin Controls (Password Protected)

Admins can log rounds manually — pick a date, select players from the full roster, add guests by name. Edit or delete existing rounds. Import attendance data from CSV for bulk loading.

Players can view everything but can't edit.

5 End Season & Archive

At year-end, the admin can archive the entire season. Archived seasons stay viewable from pill buttons in the header (e.g. "Current" | "2025 Season"). Admins can also delete archived seasons if needed.

A separate Clear All Data option permanently deletes everything — requires typing "DELETE" to confirm.

6 CSV Import

Import attendance data from a CSV file at any time. Names are automatically mapped to the Clark Group roster. Re-importing replaces the previous data, so you can refresh from an external source whenever needed.
